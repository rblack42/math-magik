Limited Pennyplane Class Rules
##############################

The Academy of Model Aeronautics publishes rules for each competition class.
Limited Pennyplane is defined in section 21 for Event 208. The basic design
constraints we must obey are these:

	* Weight - excluding motor is at least 3.10 grams

	* Overall length from most forward point on the propeller to the rear most
	  point on the aft end of the airplane must be less than 20 inches

	* The projected wingspan must be less than 18 inches.

	* Wing maximum chord is 5 inches

	* Horizontal Stabilizer maximum span is 12 inches

	* Horizontal Stabilizer maximum chord is 4 inches

	* Maximum propeller diameter is 12 inches.

All measurements for this model will use English units. OpenSCAD_ does not use
units of any kind, but by default generates SVG files that convert the
dimensionless units to centimeters. We will scale any SVG files produced so
they can be printed at proper size.
