Fuselage¶

The fuselage of our model has seven major components:

  • Motor Stick

  • Tail Boom

  • Wing posts

  • Stab posts

  • Propeller bearing

  • Rear hook

The rules call for a motor stick which is limited in length. The distance from the front of the propeller bearing to the front of the rear hook must be less than 10 inches. The main body of our airplane consists of a motor stick and a tail boom. The motor stick will support the wing, which will be attached to two posts glued to the motor stick. At the top of these posts, we will create a round segment that will plug into paper tubes attached to the leading and trailing edges of the wing. This will allow for easy transport and slight adjustments to the incidence for flight trimming.

The motor stick also supports the propeller bearing ans rear hook. This model will use a wire bearing that is glued into a slot in the front part of the motor stick.
